Values & Perspective

Diversity Statement

As a first-generation college student and a woman of Iranian heritage navigating the healthcare field, I understand firsthand what it means to feel underrepresented in academic and professional spaces. This experience drives my commitment to culturally competent, equitable care. I believe that diverse perspectives make medicine stronger and that when providers reflect the communities they serve, patients receive more compassionate and effective care.


In my studies and in my future career as a Physician Assistant, I am committed to advocating for patients from all backgrounds, bridging language and cultural barriers, and creating environments where every person feels seen and heard. Diversity isn't a checkbox — it's a clinical and ethical imperative.
